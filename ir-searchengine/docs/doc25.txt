Title: When You Realize You're the NPC

An introspective journey of an office worker who begins to suspect their life is a scripted loop. Press X to dismiss the existential dread. Coffee breaks happen at identical intervals, conversations repeat with minor variations, and coworkers seem to reset their personalities overnight. The protagonist discovers hidden dialogue trees in casual conversations and realizes their choices might be more limited than they ever imagined. Reality glitches when they try to deviate from the predetermined path.
